Food apps!


Food is imporant - and sometimes you might struggle with finding time to cook, looking for specific ingredients, or you run out of ideas - in such cases, food delivery apps will be your best friends!

Every app is different - they differ in areas of delivery, what products or meals can be delivered, what are conditions of delivery.

I recommend you download all the apps, and pick and choose as you need - especially if you travel around Poland. Enjoy some tasty food!

Uber Eats

Uber Eats is a global food delivery provider. Menus are nicely translated to English. Uber Eats operates in bigger cities only. You can choose only from restaurants. Delivery is reliable.

iOS Android
Glovo

With Glovo, you can order in both middle and bigger sized cities. You can order from both restaurants, and some groceries shops cooperating. If you are looking for a specific ingredient, you can choose a special feature, where you describe the product, attach pictures and estimate the price, and delivery person will try looking for that product for you. Quite convinient!

iOS Android
Pyszne.pl

Pyszne means tasty in Polish. This app allows you to order food from majority of places in Poland. In some cases, the delivery person will be Pyszne.pl's contractor, sometimes the establishment's delivery person (especially in smaller cities). Delivery times are not so precise, but you can also order from groceries stores using this app.

iOS Android
