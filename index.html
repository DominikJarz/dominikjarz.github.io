Here you can get to know apps that will be useful if you decide to visit or move to Poland.


To get around a city using public transport or taxis, to order some delicious food, or to book a manicure appointment, it will be useful for you to familiarize yourself with the apps presented on this website. It is a simple way to make your experience here easier - so why not!

You can also immediately download those apps to your devices using the links provided.

It is important to mention that in Poland, you will find both international and local apps for the same purpose. For example, ordering food can be done both through global Uber Eats, as well as Polish Pyszne.pl. For some purposes, you will have to stick with Polish apps.

Food!

Here you can explore the most popular food delivery applications. You will find here both the global apps working in Poland, and local ones. Choice, pricing and delivery time may vary, so it's good to be equipped with a couple of them, to always choose the best option!

COVID-19

In order to keep yourself safe during the pandemic, please familiarize yourself with a tracking app. This app is not required by the Polish law, but for the extra feeling of safety, consider it. If you already have an app from a different EU country, check if it enabled cross-border data transfer.
