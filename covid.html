COVID-19 tracking app


Technology can be really helpful with tracking COVID-19 cases. You can be notified or notify strangers you were close to about risk of catching the virus.

The tracking apps are using techniques to keep your anonymousity and do not consume little data.

You might already have an app from a different EU country - if it enabled cross-border data checks, you do not have to download a new app in order to keep yourself and others safe.

STOP COVID - ProteGO Safe

This app has been created by Ministerstwo Cyfryzacji (Ministry of Digitalization). The app supports English language.
